About

Capturing joy
in its most genuine form.

I'm Elise Nguyen, a San Jose–based photographer who believes children deserve to be photographed like the little stars they are — unscripted, joyful, and completely themselves.

Specializing in events and children, I bring a calm presence and a sharp eye to every shoot. Whether it's a first birthday, a family reunion, or a school celebration — I find the moments worth keeping.
